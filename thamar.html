01 — OVERVIEW

More than
just another project.

Thamar started with one simple question. Why is it still difficult for many local farmers to reach customers directly? Instead of treating this as just another university assignment, I wanted to design something that could solve a real problem. The result was Thamar — a platform that helps farmers showcase their products, reach more customers, and reduce dependence on intermediaries.

02 — THE PROBLEM

The problem.

Many local farmers struggle to market their products effectively. Selling through intermediaries often reduces their profits and limits direct communication with customers. At the same time, consumers have no simple place to discover trusted local farms.

03 — THE SOLUTION

The solution.

Thamar creates a digital marketplace where farmers can present their products directly to consumers. The platform focuses on simplicity, accessibility, and supporting local agriculture through technology.

04 — MY CONTRIBUTION

What I did.

Thamar is a personal project that I planned and developed from the ground up. From researching the problem and defining the idea to designing the user experience and building the platform, every stage reflects my own work and learning journey.
